Infrastructure is the first group of exhibits you will encounter on your museum visit. In the case of a widespread epidemic, most of the infrastructure in Los Angeles or your city will quickly collapse if proper measures are not put into place.

The primary purpose of these exhibits however are to help you prepare for infrastructure breakdown. After any emergency, government agencies and community leadership may not be ready in time to assist. In this case, preparing beforehand can greatly improve the community's chance of survival. Learn which type of battery is most suited for which situation and predict how long they will last. Also, see the optimal ways of interaction during times of distress.
