Exhibitions

Infrastructure

Welcome to the beginning of the end of the world! Be sure to create your group wisely. The people you are with will be with you until the very end. Don't forget to pack your batteries. You're going to need the most durable ones since you don't know when you'll be able to find more. Now go on ahead, the the end of the world ends for no one!

Major Exhibits
Social

Maps are essential during an emergency. Without a map, it is almost impossible to organize rescues or safe escapes.

After some sort of biological attack those infected with the pathogen will need to be quarantined and placed under medical care. This will require the identification of those infected.

Now that you know who in your group is infected and who isn't, you need to learn how to survive the days ahead. The downfall of society as we know will surely cause massive panic and rioting. In order to survive a riot, you must learn a set of skills that will come I handy in such a situation.

Medical

As you go through your everyday life after the day of the apocalypse, you may encounter various situations in which knowing certain medical skills may be the difference between the survival or death of someone. Learn and practice your skills in this exhibit!
