Meet the Team

Who We Are

Museum of Survival: Project Z is constructed by a group of ambitious students from the California Academy of Mathematics and Science (CAMS) for their Junior year Interdisciplinary Project (IDP). The IDP incorporates skills from every core subject into a final culminating project.

What We Did

This year, we designed a museum for IDP that revolves around emergency preparedness. We created over twelve different exhibits and ten different interactive to teach visitors the importance of preparing for an emergency by using the metaphor Zombie Apocalypse.
