Know Before You Go:
> Museum tour is separated into two categories: guided and self-guided. Self-guided viewing is not accompanied by a docent or a survival group. Guided viewing is recommend for all new visitors who want to get the full experience of Project Z.
> Photography of any exhibit is allowed! Share photos with #projectzmuseum if you'd like.
> All digital interactive exhibits are available online in the learn tab! Try to give other visitors a chance to interact by not spending too much time on them in the actual museum!

Museum Times

Please note that the Museum of Survival: Project Z will only be open for one day! This is a student developed project at the California Academy of Mathematics and Science. If you have found this site and are not aware of this project, please note that this is NOT a real museum. However, feel free to visit us on opening day if you'd like. We'll be glad to give you a tour.
